RAFIKI'S RESTAURANT AND LOUNGE

Welcome to Rafiki's restaurant for Fun galore, tasty and scrumtious Kenyan dishes, themed events and much more...come join us and meet new friends at the newly renovated Rafiki Restaurant and lounge.

We have a variety of East African dishes such as:

  • Fresh Fish served with Ugali and Greens
  • Pilau served with kachumbari or beef stew
  • Grilled goat served with Ugali or Chips
  • Flatbread(chapatti) served with beef stew
  • Kuku choma served with Ugali or Chips
  • Curry served with rice or chapati
  • Samosas
